Personal Projects

Through (Steam) - Unreal

2024

Co-Programmer

"Through" is the largest project I have completed to date, a fully online multiplayer video game published on Steam, meeting all the requirements of the largest content store on Windows and launching with both Spanish and English support. Despite this, the team behind the product was very small, consisting of only two programmers, an artist, a designer, and a sound designer.
As part of a programming duo, I handled nearly all, if not all, of the systems integrated into the project—from network communication to menu behavior, character controls, enemy AI, and much more.
Thanks to the experience gained in previous projects, I achieved excellent results in visual effects and graphical programming for this development. A standout feature is the cartoon-style post-processing, which gives the game a unique aesthetic while leveraging Unreal Engine’s capabilities, such as Lumen, to maintain dynamic light bounces even with such a stylized look.
As expected, proper replication of gameplay elements was vital in Through, so ensuring these mechanisms were correctly implemented was one of the primary tasks we programmers shared. Nearly everything implemented had to account for where it should execute (client, server, or both) and the timing of communication.
Lastly, I’d like to highlight the work I did on making the control scheme in the menus functional for both keyboard and mouse. This involved capturing and managing input in specific widgets to provide an intuitive user experience. More...
