About the game

Enter the darkness. Fight for his soul.
In a world ravaged by the Emerald Plague, death is not the end... it's only the beginning of corruption. In Tiris, you embody Liria, an alchemist and sorceress determined to defy death itself to save her beloved Narcis, turned into an abomination in the service of a dark Necromancer.

This academic project was developed by a complete team of programmers, artists, and designers. My main role was to provide support and help my teammates, in addition to implementing some features. Since it was a fairly limited project, the workload was not as high as in some of my previous projects.

Most Important Implementations


Player Movement:

This game is a twin-stick shooter, which implies a separation between the direction the character is facing and the direction of movement. Supported by Unreal Engine 5's Motion Matching system, I achieved a satisfactory control scheme that serves as the foundation of the game, applying inertia-based leaning during sharp direction changes while still providing a fast response to player input.



Additionally, the game features a climbing system that allows the protagonist to scale elevations and overcome obstacles.
